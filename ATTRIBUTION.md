## Modifications

Source information was transformed into application-ready datasets, separated into recipes, equipment, material effects, and acquisition records. Names and categories were normalized; duplicate aliases were resolved; and numerous entries were corrected or expanded using in-game observations and community verification. The resulting adapted data is distributed under [CC BY-SA 3.0](LICENSE-DATA.md).
